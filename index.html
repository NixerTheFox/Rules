🔥 Minecraft Server Rules 🔥

1. General Behavior

  • ✅ Be Respectful – No harassment, bullying, or discrimination.
  • ✅ No Hate Speech – No racism, sexism, or offensive language.
  • ✅ No Toxicity – Avoid excessive swearing or spamming.

2. Cheating & Fair Play

  • ❌ No Hacking or Exploits – No X-ray, fly hacks, or auto-clickers.
  • ❌ No Duplication or Bug Abuse.
  • ❌ No AFK Farming – No bot farming.

3. Gameplay Rules

  • 🚫 No Griefing – Respect other players' builds.
  • 🚫 No Stealing – Even in unclaimed areas.
  • 🚫 No Unwanted PvP – PvP only with consent.
  • 🚫 No Spawn Killing.

4. Chat & Communication

  • 🗣️ English Only in Global Chat.
  • 🚫 No Advertising other servers.
  • 🚫 No Spamming – No excessive caps or messages.

5. Building & Claiming

  • 🏠 Respect Land Claims – No building too close.
  • 🔥 No Lag Machines.
  • 💥 No Unwanted TNT/Lava Traps.

6. Staff & Server Rules

  • 👮 Follow Staff Instructions.
  • 📝 Report Rule Breakers in the proper channel.
  • 🚷 No Ban Evasion.

7. Economy Rules

  • 💰 No Scamming – Be honest in trades.
  • 💎 No Real-World Trading.

FTB Chunks Settings

  • Max Claimed Chunks per player: 500
  • Max Forceloaded Chunks per player: 25 (Forceloaded ALWAYS)
  • PvP in claimed chunks is team-based.
  • Claimed chunks get unclaimed after 90 days of Inactivity teams.
  • Forceloaded chunks get unloaded after 7 days of Inactivity teams .
  • Max Claimed Chunks per team: 1000

Server Settings

  • 🔄 Server restarts once a day (15min heads-up).
  • 💾 Backup occurs 30 minutes before restart.
